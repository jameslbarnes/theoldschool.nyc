An old school for new ideas
We are selecting 100 founding stewards.

The School is a 20,000-square-foot building in Greenpoint, Brooklyn. A nonprofit residency, workspace, and events institution at the intersection of art and frontier technology. One of its founders calls it “an old school for new ideas.” Artists, technologists, and researchers work side by side here on projects that wouldn't have a natural home anywhere else.

We're selecting our first 100 founding stewards: collaborators who shape the community through some mix of dues and labor. Workshops you'd lead, projects you'd run, taste you'd bring, time you'd give.

Stewardship can be money, skills, or any blend in between. To paraphrase Marshall McLuhan: there are no passengers on this spaceship. Only crew.
